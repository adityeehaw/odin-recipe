Tomato Pasta

Tomato pasta is a well known and loved dish. Tomato Pasta in Red Sauce is one of the simplest and easiest pasta to make at home. Tomato Pasta is prepared by first cooking Penne or Fusilli Pasta to Al Dente perfection and then cooking them with homemade red tomato pasta sauce.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Penne
  • Chef's kiss

Steps

  1. wash the penne and boil it till it becomes soft.
  2. make tomato puree and add spices
  3. on a pan first pour the puree, then mix the pasta
  4. cook till it starts smelling delicious :)
